üîë How to Reset Your Password

Follow these steps to securely reset your password:

  1. Navigate to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the password reset link (valid for 1 hour)
  4. Create a new strong password (minimum 12 characters)

Secure your account with our enhanced 2FA options:

Using Authenticator App
