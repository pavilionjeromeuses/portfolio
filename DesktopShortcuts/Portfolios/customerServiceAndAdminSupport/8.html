Example Scenario: Major Data Breach

SEVERITY LEVEL: CRITICAL

Incident Summary: Unauthorized access detected in customer database, potential exposure of 2M user records including payment information. Detected 03/14/24 at 22:45 EST.

Escalation Protocol

1. Detection & Reporting

Team: Security Operations Center
Actions:
- Confirm incident validity
- Log all details
- Notify Incident Manager within 30min

2. Triage & Classification

Team: Crisis Response Team
Timeline: 1 hour from detection
Output: Severity assessment, impact analysis

3. Executive Activation

Team: C-Suite & Legal
Actions:
- Approve communication plan
- Allocate resources
- Regulatory compliance check

4. Response Execution

Phase 1: Containment & Customer Notification
Phase 2: System Remediation
Phase 3: Post-Mortem & Prevention

Stakeholder Communication Matrix

Internal Stakeholders

Employees: All-hands meeting within 4hrs, daily updates via email
Board of Directors: Immediate phone briefing, detailed report within 12hrs

External Stakeholders

Customers: Email notification within 6hrs, dedicated support portal
Regulators: Formal notification per GDPR/CCPA within 24hrs
